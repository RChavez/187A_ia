Cometitive Analysis of University Websites


Produced by: Guan Wang, Melody Kim, and Ryan Chavez

This page serves to compare and contrast existing University websites in order to find what is considered state-of-the-art. The findings are used to influence the production of our redesign for the University of Louisiana, Lafayette's website.
