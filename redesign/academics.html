Academics

UL Lafayette's high academic rankings reflect its excellence in education and research. Students have the opportunity to work closely with brilliant faculty, including Nobel laureates, interact with top students, and pursue endless opportunities for personal and intellectual development. (This is from the UCSD Academics page, just changed university name)

Undergrad Degrees

UL Lafayette's rich variety of academic disciplines ensures you'll find a major that suits your skills and interests. Look into the many excellent degree programs, ranging from traditional to innovative, offered by academic departments, as well as an exciting selection of degrees offered by interdisciplinary academic programs, such as computing and the arts, earth sciences, and international studies. (This is from the UCSD Academics page, just changed university name)

Departments and Programs

UL Lafayette's academic departments offer undergraduate majors and minors taught by world-renowned faculty who engage with students in novel research projects as well as classroom-centered instruction. The ever-growing list of interdisciplinary programs features majors and minors that cross the boundaries between traditional departments and disciplines, giving students opportunities to study topics that reflect the dynamic collaborations across the various disciplines of the campus. (This is from the UCSD Academics page, just changed university name)

Graduate Programs

UL, Lafayette offers advanced and professional degrees in a variety of disciplines, from science and technology, social science and the humanities, to medicine and business. Join us for a challenging and rewarding education in your field of interest. (This is from the UCSD Academics page, just changed university name)
