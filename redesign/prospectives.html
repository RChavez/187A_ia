  • PROSPECTIVE STUDENTS
  • University of Louisiana, Lafayette among top-ranked universities in the nation for positive impact on the country (Washington Monthly's College Guide). UL is among the best public universities in the nation.

  • Undergraduate Students
    • Are you a high-achieving student looking for a world-class education? Start here! Whether you're ready to fill out the application or planning ahead, we've got information you need.

  • Graduate Students
    • Interested in a post-graduate education? UL is a powerhouse research university and offers first professional degrees as well as academic degrees at the Master and Doctoral levels.

  • Professional Students
    • On-campus professional schools cross disciplinary boundaries, just like our undergraduate programs. Many classes are open to undergraduates, too.

  • International Students
    • Intercultural participation enriches our campus community. Students and international scholars take classes, pursue research, and teach on campus.
